Chourouk

School

Chourouk School is a school that has more than one branch around Morocco. It is a private primary and secondary school. It has 3 branches in Meknes: "Chourouk 1", "Chourouk 2", and "Chourouk 3". I studied my primary education at "Chourouk 2" School and my secondary education at "Chourouk 3".

This school also has the continuation of series 1, 2, 3 which is 4 in Al Hajeb.

Chourouk has 3 accounts on Facebook where they post their activities, outings, or many announcements about them... , One account includes all the branches, the second is for "Chourouk 3" and the last account is for "Chourouk 4".
