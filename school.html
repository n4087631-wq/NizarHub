My study

status

My study status was good to some extent, as throughout primary school it was excellent and sometimes more so, and while I was making this site, I was in the second year of middle school. Throughout middle school so far, my level is good, which makes me proud. But I may not excel academically during these periods because I do not study what I want and I do not study my interests, but in any case, I will study well until I reach the level at which I will study my interests.

My

school

I studied for about 8 years in a private school called "Chorouk" and I will finish my last year, which is the 9th year, next year. Chorouk School is an expanding school and has branches all over Morocco. Do you want to know more about it?

First

Year

The first year, which was the first elementary school, was the year I entered this school, as I did not know anyone and no one knew me because most of them studied with each other in kindergarten the previous year, but it was not a problem because with time I got used to them and became part of them. I may not assess my level in that year accurately, but I say that I was one of the best, I believe.
